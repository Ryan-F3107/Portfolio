About me

Hello ! I am a soon to be graduate student from Queen's University, obtaining a Bachelor of Computing(Honours) Degree specializing in Software Design. Throughout my education and work experience, I realized that I enjoy the process of bringing people's & my own ideas to life using various resources. Initially, I joined the computing program with the thought of game dev as my chosen path but soon realized that I enjoyed the possibility of working with various industries as a software developer and so I made the change.

Being in a field where I have the option to choose various roles/positions and being a self motivated individual eager to learn, I am thrilled to explore my options, learning various technology skills along the way. A career milestone or personal achievement of mine, is to be part of a process/product development that takes various industries to the next level or working towards a sustainable future.
